Our Story & Vision

Founded in 2003, Compass Housing Society has grown from a small real estate venture to one of the most trusted names in premium residential development. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create homes that inspire and communities that thrive.

Over two decades, we've transformed urban landscapes with innovative designs, sustainable practices, and unwavering commitment to quality. Today, we manage over 50 residential communities and have delivered homes to more than 10,000 satisfied families.
